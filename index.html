Making Biogas SMART using Internet of Things(IoT)

It's time to bring a change!

The Idea

This project proposes to increase the productivity and to monitor the usage of biogas by monitoring and controling the pH value as well as the water level. We will also be monitoring and calulating the bill of total biogas consumption.
The purpose is also to spread awareness of causes of stubble burning , how to dispose of their stubble, keep bio degradable and non-bio degradable waste in different dustbins.
We will provide information about how much money the farmers will get by selling their stubble, animal manures and other bio-degradable waste; how slurry cab be used as fertilizers, labour to collect the stubble.

How is it beneficial ?.

Productivity of BioGas

It is a scalable solution to the increase the productivity, to monitor the usage and to spread awareness about biogas.

Side Income and employement creation.

It will create a side income for the farmers and futher create more employement options for people.

Reduction in Greenhouse Gases

It will reduce the greenhouse gases and also reduce the air pollution.

Alternative to LPG

It is a better alternative to LPG.BIOGAS is more safer than CNG and 3 times more safer than LPG Cylinder.

We are Proud to say that with 1 time investment in the plant and with working capital(of stubble and animal manure), the proposed system of ours will be a Beneficial Business Model.
